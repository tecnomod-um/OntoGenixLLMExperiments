**Data Description:**
1. Company: text - The name of the company involved in the complaint - [1:M]
2. Company public response: categorical - Public response provided by the company - [0:M]
3. Company response to consumer: categorical - Response given by the company to the consumer - [1:M]
4. Consumer complaint narrative: text - Detailed narrative of the complaint submitted by the consumer - [0:M]
5. Consumer consent provided?: categorical - Indicates if consumer consent was provided for data sharing - [0:M]
6. Consumer disputed?: categorical - Indicates if the consumer disputed the complaint resolution - [0:0]
7. Date received: text - The date on which the complaint was received - [1:M]
8. Date sent to company: text - The date on which the complaint was forwarded to the company - [1:M]
9. Issue: text - The issue raised in the complaint - [1:M]
10. Product: categorical - The type of product the complaint is about - [1:M]
11. State: text - The state from which the complaint was filed - [0:M]
12. Sub-issue: text - Detailed issue related to the main issue - [0:M]
13. Sub-product: text - Detailed product related to the main product - [1:M]
14. Submitted via: categorical - The medium through which the complaint was submitted - [1:M]
15. Tags: categorical - Tags associated with the complaint - [0:M]
16. Timely response?: categorical - Indicates if the response to the complaint was timely - [1:M]
17. Complaint ID: Numerical - Unique identifier for the complaint - [1:1]
18. ZIP code: Numerical - ZIP code related to the consumer's location - [0:M]
