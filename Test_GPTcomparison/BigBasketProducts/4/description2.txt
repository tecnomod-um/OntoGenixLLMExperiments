1. Absolute_Url: text - The full URL of a product - Unique for each product
2. Brand: text - The brand name associated with a product - Multiple products can have the same brand
3. Category: text - The category to which a product belongs - Limited to a small set of categories
4. Image_Url: text - The URL of the product's image - Almost unique for each product
5. ProductName: text - The name of the product - Multiple products can have similar names
6. Quantity: text - The quantity in stock for a product - Multiple products can have the same quantity
7. SubCategory: text - The subcategory to which a product belongs - Multiple products can belong to the same subcategory
8. DiscountPrice: Numerical - The discounted price of a product - Ranges from 3 to 7605 with a mean of 356.9079678363
9. Price: Numerical - The original price of a product - Ranges from 5 to 10140 with a mean of 545.4185891813
