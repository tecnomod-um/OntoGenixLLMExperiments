**Data Description:**
1. Absolute_Url: text - The full URL of a product or item - Unique URLs
2. Brand: text - The brand name associated with a product - Multiple products per brand
3. Category: text - The general category to which a product belongs - Limited to a set of categories
4. Image_Url: text - The URL of an image associated with a product - Unique or nearly unique URLs
5. ProductName: text - The name of the product - Multiple products can share a name
6. Quantity: text - The quantity of the product available or sold - Various quantities
7. SubCategory: text - A more specific category within the general category - Multiple products per subcategory
8. DiscountPrice: Numerical - The discounted price of a product - Range from minimum to maximum observed values
9. Price: Numerical - The original price of a product - Range from minimum to maximum observed values
