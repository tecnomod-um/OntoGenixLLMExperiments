**Data Description:**
1. ProductId: text - Identifier for products - Unique
2. Rating: categorical - Assessment score of a product - 1 to 5
3. Timestamp: text - Date and time when the rating was recorded - Unique
4. UserId: text - Identifier for users - Unique

**classes:**
1. Product
2. User
3. Review

**subclasses:**
1. RatedProduct: subclass of -> Product
2. Reviewer: subclass of -> User
3. ProductReview: subclass of -> Review

**Object Properties:**
1. hasReview: domain - User; range - Review
2. reviewedProduct: domain - Review; range - Product
3. reviewer: domain - Review; range - User

**Data Type Properties:**
1. productId: domain - Product; range - xsd:string
2. ratingValue: domain - Review; range - xsd:integer
3. timestampValue: domain - Review; range - xsd:dateTime
4. userId: domain - User; range - xsd:string
