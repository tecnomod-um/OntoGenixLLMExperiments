Missing ending dots in the prefixes. Example:

@prefix owl: <http://www.w3.org/2002/07/owl#>


Incorrect use of the ending dots in the statements, and missing semicolons. Example:

<http://base_ontology.com#has_absolute_url>
  rdf:type owl:ObjectProperty.
  rdfs:domain <http://base_ontology.com#class_entity>.
  rdfs:range xsd:string.


Incorrect statements:
<http://base_ontology.com#entity_name>
  rdfs:subClassOf <http://base_ontology.com#class_entity>.
  owl:oneOf
    (
      xsd:string
    ).
