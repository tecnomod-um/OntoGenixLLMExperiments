Define it as an owl type

**Data Description:**
1. Absolute_Url: text - The full URL of a product - Unique for each product
2. Brand: text - The brand name associated with a product - Multiple products can have the same brand
3. Category: text - The general category of a product - Limited to a small set of categories
4. Image_Url: text - The URL of the product's image - Almost unique for each product
5. ProductName: text - The name of the product - Multiple products can have similar names
6. Quantity: text - The quantity in stock for a product - Can vary widely across products
7. SubCategory: text - A more specific category within the general category - Multiple products can belong to the same subcategory
8. DiscountPrice: Numerical - The discounted price of a product - Ranges from a minimum of 3 to a maximum of 7605
9. Price: Numerical - The original price of a product - Ranges from a minimum of 5 to a maximum of 10140

**classes:**
1. Product

**subclasses:**
1. Brand: subclass of -> Product
2. Category: subclass of -> Product
3. SubCategory: subclass of -> Category

**Object Properties:**
1. hasBrand: domain - Product; range - Brand
2. hasCategory: domain - Product; range - Category
3. hasSubCategory: domain - Category; range - SubCategory
4. hasImage: domain - Product; range - Image_Url
